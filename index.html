Features

Everything You Loved,
We brought back

Here are all of the planned features for our server — no subscriptions, no catch.

Your Worlds, Preserved

Player-built rooms, inventions, and custom content — all restored and kept online. Your creations have a permanent home.

Play With Friends

Multiplayer, voice chat, and full social features intact. Find your crew and jump back in together, just like old times.

All Game Modes

Paintball, Laser Tag, Disc Golf, Quests, Jumbotron — all the classic modes you remember, still running strong.

VR & Flatscreen

Full support for Meta Quest, PCVR, and non-VR flatscreen players. No headset required — everyone's welcome.

Open Source

Built transparently by the community. Audit the code, contribute fixes, or help shape the roadmap. RecUnited belongs to everyone.

Safe & Moderated

Community-elected moderators keep RecUnited a welcoming space. The same inclusive spirit Rec Room was known for.
