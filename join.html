How to Play

Follow these steps to redirect your client to the RecUnited servers.

1

Join the Discord

Everything starts on Discord. That's where we post announcements, server status updates, and links to the launch files. It's also the best spot to find a squad.

Join Discord
2

Get the Patcher

Head to the #downloads channel in our Discord and grab the RecUnited patcher. Running this automatically tells your local copy of the game to bypass the official servers and connect to us.

3

Make an Account

When you start up for the first time, you'll need to create a new profile. Since official data is lost, we all start fresh. Customize your avatar, pick a name, and jump into the RecCenter.
