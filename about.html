Preserving The Game

RecUnited was born entirely out of necessity. We believe gaming communities shouldn't just vanish when servers get shut down.

Our Mission

Our raw goal is to accurately recreate and host the multiplayer backend needed to keep Rec Room alive indefinitely. We are passionate fans who spent thousands of hours in-game.

We are building a safe, moderated, and entirely free alternative so that years of player-created rooms and memories do not vanish into the ether when the big corporation pulls the plug.

Server Status

The servers are currently IN DEVELOPMENT. Our team is actively building the backend infrastructure required to get everyone back into the game! Keep an eye on our Discord for updates.
